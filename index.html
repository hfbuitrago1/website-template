Felipe Buitrago

Universidad de los Andes

Hi there! I am a senior majoring in Psychology with a minor in Computational Mathematics at the Universidad de los Andes.

My research interests lie in the intersection of socioeconomic inequality, social dynamics, and quantitative methods. In particular, my research focuses on the relationship between individual behavior and socioeconomic inequality from a network perspective. Specifically, I am interested in advancing the understanding of the structural properties of social networks under which socioeconomic inequality arises when individuals interact in social groups.

I was a Research Fellow and a Research Assistant in the Department of Psychology at Cornell University in 2019 - 2020. I also received a BA in Philosophy from Universidad de los Andes in 2021.
