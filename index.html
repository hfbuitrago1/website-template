Felipe Buitrago

Universidad de los Andes

I am a senior majoring in Psychology with a minor in Computational Mathematics at Universidad de los Andes.

My research focuses on the relationship between individuals' behavior and socioeconomic inequality from a network perspective. In particular, I am interested in advancing the understanding of the structural properties of social networks under which socioeconomic inequality arises when individuals interact in social groups.

I was a research fellow and a research assistant in the Department of Psychology at Cornell University. I also earned a BA in Philosophy cum laude from Universidad de los Andes in 2021.
